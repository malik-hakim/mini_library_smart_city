Smart Living

Smart Living is a system that aims to support the quality of life of city residents in an inclusive manner. starting from providing modern public facilities, digital-based health services, developing green spaces, to technology-based city security systems. The goal is to create a better community life with easy access to public services, creating a healthy and safe environment, as well as sustainability that supports the welfare of current and future generations.

Success Indicators

Another indicator of a smart city is the creation of smart living. Smart living is an important aspect in the development of a smart city which focuses on improving people's quality of life through the use of information and communication technology. Through this concept, people are expected to be able to enjoy a healthier, safer and more comfortable living environment with the support of an integrated digital system. For example, the existence of digital-based health services makes it easier for people to access health information, carry out remote consultations with doctors, and utilize early warning systems to maintain safety.

Apart from that, smart living also includes providing housing facilities that are adequate, environmentally friendly and efficient in energy use. With a smart residential system, people can manage the use of electricity, water and home security automatically through technological devices. On the other hand, implementing smart living also involves building public spaces that are inclusive, friendly to all groups, and support social interaction between residents. In this way, smart living not only increases the comfort of life, but also strengthens social relations and encourages the creation of a more productive and harmonious society.
