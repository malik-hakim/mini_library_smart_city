Smart City Principle Benefit Goals

What Is a Smart City?

A Smart City is a concept that integrates advanced technologies to improve various aspects of urban life, such as transportation, energy, the environment, and public services. Supported by information and communications technology (ICT), smart cities are able to efficiently connect systems and services.

Smart City Principles

Integration of Information and Communication Technology (ICT)
Efficiency and Sustainability
Focus on Citizen Welfare
Active Community Participation

Benefits of Smart City

Public Service Efficiency: With digitalization, administrative and government services become faster and more transparent..

Better Resource Management: Technology can be used to control the use of electricity, water, and energy to become more efficient.

Increased Security: With the existence of IoT and CCTV-based monitoring systems, city security can be increased.

Improved Mobility and Public Well-Being: Smart transportation systems with real-time navigation can help reduce congestion and improve travel comfort. Furthermore, access to healthcare, education, and information becomes more accessible and equitable for all levels of society.
